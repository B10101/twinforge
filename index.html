Who we are

Twinforge Technologies, embodying its motto 'Audax et Egregie Innovationes' (“Bold and Brilliant Innovations”), is a forward-thinking engineering startup founded by JKUAT graduates.

Leveraging expertise in robotics, AI, and additive manufacturing, the company seeks to revolutionize industries with innovative and practical solutions that address modern challenges. Specializing in machinery enhancement, automation, and advanced safety features, Twinforge provides scalable, custom solutions tailored to SMEs, educational institutions, and manufacturers. By aligning with Industry 4.0 principles, the company bridges traditional engineering practices with cutting-edge technologies to meet evolving demands. With a vision for bold innovation and impactful transformation, Twinforge Technologies is committed to shaping a sustainable, technology-driven future. The company invites partners and clients to join its journey to redefine the engineering landscape and pioneer exceptional innovations that leave a lasting impact
